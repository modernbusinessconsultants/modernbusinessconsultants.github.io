Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to what most business owners ask us.

What size business is MBC built for? +
We work best with small businesses that have 2-50 employees. If you're big enough to have problems but too small to hire a full IT department, finance team, or marketing agency, we're your people.
Do I need to sign a long-term contract? +
No. Our plans are month-to-month. We earn your business every single month. If we're not delivering value, you can cancel anytime.
What's the difference between Growth and Accelerator? +
Growth adds marketing and SEO hygiene on top of your tech foundation. Accelerator goes further by adding fractional finance and operations leadership. Think of it as hiring a COO and CFO without the six-figure salaries.
How fast can you get started? +
Most clients are fully onboarded within one week. We audit your current systems, set up monitoring, and start delivering value from day one.
