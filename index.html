Questions we get a lot.

What size businesses do you work with? +
Mostly small to mid-size businesses with 1-50 employees. If you're too small for a full-time IT person, accountant, or marketing team but too big to keep winging it — you're exactly who we built this for.
Do I have to buy all your services? +
No. Start with what hurts the most. Most clients begin with IT or bookkeeping and add services as they see results. There's no bundle requirement.
Is there a contract or commitment? +
No long-term contracts. We work month to month. We keep clients because we deliver results, not because of legal fine print. Custom software projects are scoped individually.
What does "per seat" mean? +
One seat = one employee. If someone uses a laptop and a desktop, that's still one seat. We manage the person's entire tech footprint, not individual machines.
Can you really build custom apps? +
Yes. We've built lead-generating CRMs, inventory management systems, automated SMS follow-ups on missed calls, and more. If you have a workflow that's held together by spreadsheets and sticky notes, we can turn it into real software.
What makes you different from other MSPs or agencies? +
Most IT companies don't touch your books. Most marketing agencies don't manage your laptops. Most bookkeepers can't build you a website. We do all of it under one roof, with two people who actually know your business — not a rotating cast of outsourced support reps.
